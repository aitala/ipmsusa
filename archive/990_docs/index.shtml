IPMS/USA IRS 990 Policies

IPMS/USA has outgrown filing taxes under IRS Form 990-EZ, used by 501(c ) organizations. As many of these organizations are true fund-raising non-profits, the IRS tends to also look at their management and governance, both in terms of questions on the form, and existing policies. IPMS/USA now falls under this umbrella and the E-Board has prepared and approved two policies -- Conflict of Interest and Whistleblower -- to enhance our tax compliance and serve the membership.
